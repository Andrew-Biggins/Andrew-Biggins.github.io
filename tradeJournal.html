Trade Journal

  • </> Code

Trade Journal is tool for recording trades in the financial markets and reviewing and fine-tuning trading performance. Trades can be added, removed and edited; open trades can be included and updated when they are closed.

Filters can be used to used to generate metrics on subsets of trades to what is working well and what is working not so well. Filters can be individual or groups markets or strategies, or specific date ranges or day of the week amongst others. Trades can also be sorted by any field by clicking the desired column header.

Note: open trades are not included for the metric statistics calculations.
