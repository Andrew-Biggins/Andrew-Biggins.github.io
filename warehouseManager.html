Warehouse Manager

Code is private as requested by UWE, contact me for a sample.

The warehouse manager app enables finding storage space for a list of new items across multiple warehouses based on the item's shape and value. It makes the most economic use of the space available and rejects any items that cannot be accommodated.

Items can be relocated with routes being planned to keep within the transportation van's weight, insurance and daily distance limits.

The inventories for each warehouse can be viewed at any time, and all items can be restored to their original location if desired.
